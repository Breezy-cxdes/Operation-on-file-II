The quest for knowledge has always illuminated the dawn of civilization, and at the heart of this quest lies the enigma of Artificial Intelligence (AI). The journey of AI is a riveting tale of human ambition, ingenuity, and the relentless pursuit of transcending cognitive frontiers. From the nascent inklings in ancient myths to the sophisticated algorithms of today, AI has traversed a remarkable trajectory, metamorphosing from a fantastical notion into a reality that is as enthralling as it is formidable. This post dives into the roots of AI, tracing its evolutionary trajectory from myth to machine, and casts a speculative eye into what lies ahead in the AI odyssey.
